Welcome to Adaptive Learning 2.0

There are five different Learning Strategies in the Adaptive Learning 2.0 system. The Learning Strategy Test allows the system to assess what Learning Strategy will be best for you. After taking the Learning Strategy test you will be placed in the Learning Strategy best suited for you based on the Learning Strategy test results.

  • Apprentice

    Step-by-step procedural learning. Content is presented in a systematic step-by-step procedural way so a student can learn by following simple steps.

  • Incidental

    Events in the story or case-study with role playing. Content is presented in real-life scenarios or case studies and the "events" in the story trigger the learning experience. These events lead to incidental learning.

  • Inductive

    Numerous examples that confirm to generalized principles. Content is presented with examples that illustrate the principle clearly to a student. The examples enable students to develop their own understanding of the concept.

  • Deductive

    Principles leading to further trends and parametric variations. Content is presented with interactive animations that allow the student to learn by doing interactive tasks that lead to the understanding of a concept.

  • Discovery

    Experiments leading to data and data leading to a discovery or a principle. Content is presented in a simulated learning environment where a student can interact with their virtual learning environment to conduct his own discovery of a concept.

Your assigned Learning Strategy may change based on statistical data and is driven by the Practice Quizzes. You may be rotated through different strategies until the system convenes on the one strategy that you perform best with. The assigned Learning Strategy may change as your performance changes and may change for different content.

You can always change your current Learning Strategy manually by using the Current Strategy drop-down menu in the top navigation bar. The system will remember your choice as you move on to other pages and topics in the course content.

You can see a visualization of your Learning Strategy progress at any time by clicking on the My Acheivements link in the drop-down menu with your name on it. You are not required to achieve 100% in each Learning Strategy but if you do passing the Assessments should be no problem!
